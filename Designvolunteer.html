Design volunteer

About:

Green Occasion curates joyful celebrations with lasting impact. Driven by sustainability and love for greenery, we're your partner in meaningful, eco-conscious moments that bloom beyond the event. Join us!

Position Overview :

We are seeking a talented Design Volunteer to join our team. As a volunteer, you will play a crucial role in creating engaging visual content that supports our environmental projects and campaigns. This is an excellent opportunity to use your design expertise to make a meaningful impact on environmental sustainability.

Responsibilities

  • Collaborate with the marketing and project teams to understand design requirements and objectives.
  • Create visually appealing graphics, infographics, social media content, and marketing materials.
  • Design layouts for reports, presentations, and other documents.
  • Develop branding materials that align with our mission and values.
  • Ensure all designs are consistent with our brand guidelines.
  • Participate in brainstorming sessions and contribute creative ideas.
  • Stay updated with the latest design trends and tools.
  • Provide support for web and UX/UI design as needed.

Requirements

  • Proven experience as a Graphic Designer, Visual Designer, or similar role.
  • Proficiency in design software such as Adobe Creative Suite (Illustrator, Photoshop, InDesign) or similar tools.
  • Strong portfolio showcasing your design skills and creativity.
  • Understanding of visual design principles and typography.
  • Ability to create compelling and aesthetically pleasing designs.
  • Excellent attention to detail and organizational skills.
  • Passion for environmental conservation and sustainability.
  • Ability to work independently and collaboratively in a remote setting.
  • Strong communication skills.

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Experience with web design and UX/UI principles.
  • Knowledge of video editing and animation software (e.g., Adobe After Effects, Premiere Pro).
  • Prior involvement in green technology projects or environmental campaigns.
  • Familiarity with social media platforms and content creation for digital marketing.

Benefits:

  • Opportunity to contribute to impactful environmental projects.
  • Gain experience working with a passionate and diverse team.
  • Enhance your portfolio with volunteer experience in the green sector.
  • Flexible remote working environment.
  • Personal satisfaction from making a positive difference for the planet.

If you are enthusiastic about using your design skills to support environmental sustainability, we would love to hear from you! Please send your resume, a brief cover letter explaining your interest in this volunteer position, then apply for this job.
