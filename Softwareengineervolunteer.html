Software engineer volunteer

About:

Green Occasion curates joyful celebrations with lasting impact. Driven by sustainability and love for greenery, we're your partner in meaningful, eco-conscious moments that bloom beyond the event. Join us!

Position Overview :

We are seeking a dedicated Software Engineer volunteer to join our green initiatives team. As a volunteer, you will have the opportunity to develop software solutions that support our environmental projects, ranging from carbon footprint calculators to platforms that encourage sustainable practices. This is a unique chance to use your technical skills for a meaningful cause.

Responsibilities

  • Collaborate with the project team to understand requirements and objectives.
  • Develop, test, and deploy software applications and tools focused on environmental sustainability.
  • Write clean, maintainable, and efficient code.
  • Troubleshoot, debug, and upgrade existing software solutions.
  • Document development processes, code, and software functionalities.
  • Stay updated with the latest industry trends and technologies related to software development and environmental sustainability.

Requirements

  • Proven experience as a Software Engineer or similar role.
  • Strong knowledge of programming languages such as Python, JavaScript, html , css.
  • Experience with web development frameworks (e.g., Django, Flask, React, Node.js).
  • Familiarity with database management (e.g., MySQL, PostgreSQL, MongoDB).
  • Passion for environmental conservation and sustainability.
  • Excellent problem-solving skills and attention to detail.
  • Ability to work independently and collaboratively in a remote setting.
  • Strong communication skills.

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Experience with cloud services (e.g., AWS, Google Cloud, Azure).
  • Knowledge of environmental data analysis and visualization.
  • Prior involvement in green technology projects or initiatives.
  • Understanding of agile development methodologies.

Benefits:

  • Opportunity to contribute to impactful environmental projects.
  • Gain experience working with a passionate and diverse team.
  • Enhance your resume with volunteer experience in the green tech sector.
  • Flexible remote working environment.
  • Personal satisfaction from making a positive difference for the planet.

If you are enthusiastic about using your design skills to support environmental sustainability, we would love to hear from you! Please send your resume, a brief cover letter explaining your interest in this volunteer position, then apply for this job.
